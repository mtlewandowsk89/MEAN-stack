Tools of the trade

Every artist needs their chosen tools to create a masterpiece. Here's what we recommend to get started:
  • Locking tongs
  • Metal spatula
  • Rubber spatula
  • Slotted spoon
  • Whisk
  • Chef's knife (8 to 9 inch blade)
  • Grater
  • Serrated bread knife
  • Can opener
  • Thermometer
  • Measuring cups
  • Measuring spoons
  • Timer
  • Cutting board (wooden is preferred)
  • Mixing bowl
  • Nonstick frying pan
  • Nonstick sauce pan
  • Rimmed baking sheet
  • Pot holders or oven mitts
Not all of these are required for every recipe, but if you have all these tools in your kitchen you will be prepared to make any recipe you can imagine.
